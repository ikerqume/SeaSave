Volunteer Program

Our volunteers are the soul of SeaSave. We have different programs to adapt to your availability and skills:

Field Volunteering

  • Beach and seabed cleanup: Participate in our periodic waste removal activities.
  • Census and monitoring: Help collect data on marine species and ecosystem status.
  • Wildlife rescue: Be part of our rapid response team to attend emergencies with marine animals.

Job opportunities

We periodically open selection processes to incorporate new talents into our team. The currently available positions are:

Field Technician - Posidonia (Part-time)

We are looking for a marine biologist or environmental scientist to participate in our posidonia meadow conservation project.

Requirements: Higher degree in Marine Biology or Environmental Sciences, field work experience, diving certification (minimum PADI Advanced).

Communications Manager (Full-time)

We need a communication professional to coordinate our media and social network strategy.

Requirements: Degree in Communication or Journalism, minimum 2 years experience in NGOs or environmental projects, proficiency in Catalan, Spanish and English.
