About Us

Meet the team behind SeaSave and our story

Our Story

SeaSave was born in early 2025 from the shared passion of a group of enthusiasts and activists concerned about the accelerated degradation of marine ecosystems. Although we are a young organization, we have rapidly grown to become an emerging reference in ocean protection in Spain.

Since our foundation, we have developed several pioneering conservation, research, and environmental education projects, always aiming to preserve marine biodiversity and raise awareness about the importance of the oceans for life on our planet.

Our Values

  • Commitment: Full dedication to protecting and conserving marine ecosystems.
  • Transparency: Clear and honest communication about our activities and funding.
  • Collaboration: Networking with other organizations, institutions, and local communities.
  • Scientific Rigor: Our actions are based on research and verified data.
  • Education: We believe awareness and training are key to change.
