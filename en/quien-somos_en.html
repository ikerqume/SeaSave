About Us

Meet the team behind SeaSave and our story

Our Story

SeaSave was born in early 2025 from the shared passion of a group of enthusiasts and activists concerned about the accelerated deterioration of marine ecosystems. Although we are a young organization, we have rapidly grown to become an emerging reference in ocean protection in Spain.

Since our founding, we have developed several pioneering projects in conservation, research, and environmental education, always with the goal of preserving marine biodiversity and raising awareness about the importance of oceans for life on our planet.

Our Values

  • Commitment: Total dedication to the protection and conservation of marine ecosystems.
  • Transparency: Clear and honest communication about our activities and funding.
  • Collaboration: Network work with other organizations, institutions, and local communities.
  • Scientific rigor: Our actions are based on verified research and data.
  • Education: We believe that awareness and training are key to change.

Recognition and Collaborations

Despite our short trajectory, we have established strategic alliances with various institutions and have begun to be recognized for our commitment to marine conservation:

Awards and Distinctions

  • 2025: Finalists in the Marine Conservation Innovation Award granted by the Ministry for Ecological Transition.

Institutional Collaborations

We work closely with:

  • University of Barcelona - Marine Ecology Department
  • Spanish National Research Council (CSIC)
  • Generalitat de Catalunya - Department of Territory and Sustainability
  • International Ocean Protection Network (IOPN)
  • Valencia Oceanographic
  • Various coastal municipalities in Catalonia and Valencia Community

Initial Achievements

  • Launch of a pilot project to clean 2 kilometers of Mediterranean coast.
  • First steps to create a citizen surveillance network for marine ecosystems on the Costa Brava.
  • Collaboration in a scientific study on microplastics in the Mediterranean.
