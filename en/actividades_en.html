What we are doing

At SeaSave we actively work to combat plastic pollution in our seas and protect marine biodiversity through various initiatives and activities.

Our Main Activities

Beach Cleanups

We organize monthly cleanup days at main beaches. Next date: May 25, 2025

Educational Workshops

We bring workshops about plastic impact to schools. Led by: Aisha Delgado

Marine Research

We study the impact of microplastics on marine species. Coordinated by: Iker Quesada

Campaign #PlasticFreeSeas

Our annual awareness campaign seeks to promote the reduction of single-use plastics. We participate in local events, fairs and give informative talks.
